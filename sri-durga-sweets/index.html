Frequently Asked Questions

Any Questions? We have Answers

Learn more about our ingredients, ordering process, and shelf life.

Do your pickles contain preservatives?

▼
No, our pickles are completely free of artificial preservatives, vinegar, or artificial food coloring. We use cold-pressed groundnut oil, salt, and dry spices which act as natural preservatives, just like traditional homemade pickles.

What is the shelf life of your pickles and podulu?

▼
Our Veg Pickles last for up to 6 months if kept in a cool, dry place and handled with a clean, dry spoon. Non-Veg Pickles are best consumed within 3 months (refrigeration is recommended to retain freshness). Podulu (spice blends) have a shelf life of 4-6 months.

How does the WhatsApp ordering process work?

▼
Once you add items to the cart and fill in your delivery details, our checkout tool compiles the details and opens a WhatsApp conversation with us. We will confirm your order, calculate delivery estimates, and share GPay/PhonePe details for payment. Once paid, we ship your order!

Do you ship outside of Kurnool/Andhra Pradesh?

▼
Yes, we ship pan-India! We deliver to Hyderabad, Bangalore, Chennai, Mumbai, Pune, and almost all cities across India via reliable courier partners. Shipping is FREE on orders above ₹499.
